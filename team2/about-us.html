Our Goal :

We believe that when teachers think like designers they create students who think like designers.

What we do

  • We put teachers through a "crash course" of being creative and curious.
  • During this time we teach them how to create something like a website or a board game (see events).
  • Then at the end of the week, we bring their students in and they create a website or a board game together.
