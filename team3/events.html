​A Systems Thinking Workshop

How did Doc Brown and Marty McFly travel from the past to create a better future? They understood systems and made connections. We can’t predict the future, but we can equip students to navigate and solve complex challenges.

Participants in this workshop will learn the basics of Systems Thinking and Design Process and will develop a design challenge for students. The workshop includes a Lab where teachers pilot their design challenge with a group of students to get immediate feedback on their instructional design.

Think Like a Filmmaker: A Design Thinking Workshop

July 18 - 22 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM William Smith High School, Aurora, CO

Filmmaking is a complex and rewarding process that challenges teams to plan, create, and revise in order to convey a message. Teachers can use film to help students learn the Creative Process and develop essential higher order thinking skills.
