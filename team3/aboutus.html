About us

Studio Learning Labs is an organization committed to shifting mindsets, empowering teachers, and helping students create the lives they want to live. We believe that teachers who think like designers create students who think like designers. We create the space and offer opportunities for teachers to design learning experiences that will engage and inspire their students and prepare them for the future.
