Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay for PLEXUS?

Participation in the PLEXUS network is free of charge. Our partners pay a fee, which covers the majority of our operational expenses. Philanthropic dollars cover the rest.

Do I need to be attending school to be a part of PLEXUS?

School - or no school - you are a learner with great potential. At this stage, beacuse of the partnerships we have cultivated, we welcome all learners, ages 17-21, wherever they may be in their learning journey.

How long can I be a part of PLEXUS?

As noted in our strategy, our goal is to launch you into an inspiring and satisfying life path. We are commited to your success. We know every learner is diffrent and the only opportunity to accomplish great things takes time. We work with each learner and their family in setting appropriate goals.
