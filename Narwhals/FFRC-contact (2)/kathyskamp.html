2017 EARLY REGISTRATION OPEN

FFRC's summer camp program, named Kathy's Kamp after our founding
director, is a high-quality summer program designed to provide affordable
childcare for working families with school-age children, while
simultaneously exposing participating children to a traditional summer camp
program that they might not otherwise ever experience.
